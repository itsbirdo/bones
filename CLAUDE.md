Project: **BONES**, a 1940s-noir Cee-lo dice roguelike (Unity, mobile). See [README.md](./README.md)
and [GAME_DESIGN_SPEC.md](./GAME_DESIGN_SPEC.md) for the design. Roadmap and status live in
[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/itsbirdo/bones/issues) (epics by area).

## Copy & writing rules

- **Never use em dashes (—) in any copy text.** This applies to all player-facing copy (game UI,
  result shouts, narrative/script in [NARRATIVE.md](./NARRATIVE.md)) and project docs (README, marketing).
  Use commas, colons, parentheses, or shorter sentences instead.
